About DIHS

Dasmariñas Integrated High School, formerly known as Dasmariñas National High School, established in 1975. Located in Burol 1, City of Dasmariñas, Cavite, dedicated to providing an excellent education in a supportive environment. Explore our web page to learn more about our campus, programs, and the vibrant community that defines our school. Join us on this journey of knowledge and growth at Dasmariñas Integrated High School.

Benefits

Efficient Navigation

Navigate through the school campus effortlessly with the integrated map feature, ensuring students, staff, and visitors reach their destinations without hassle.

Instant Announcements

Receive real-time announcements and updates directly on your device, keeping everyone informed about important events, deadlines, and emergency situations.

Up-to-Date News

Stay informed about the latest school news and happenings with the Nabee Application's news feed, keeping the school community connected and engaged.

Access the school calendar at your fingertips, allowing users to stay updated on upcoming events, holidays, exams, and extracurricular activities.

Students can easily access study materials, class schedules, and educational resources through the Nabee Application, enhancing their learning experience and promoting academic success.
